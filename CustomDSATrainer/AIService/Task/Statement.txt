Title: Elementary Addition

Statement:
Given two non-negative integers a and b, compute their sum.

Constraints:
0 <= a, b <= 1000

Example 1:
Input:
1
2
Output:
3
Explanation:
1 + 2 = 3

Example 2:
Input:
5
5
Output:
10
Explanation:
5 + 5 = 10

Example 3:
Input:
100
0
Output:
100
Explanation:
100 + 0 = 100
